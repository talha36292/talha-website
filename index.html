About

I am a PhD Scholar at IIIT-Delhi, working with Dr. Anuj Grover on SRAM memory architectures and modeling for compute-in-memory (CIM). My research spans bitcell-level circuit design, sense-amplifier characterization, peripheral I/O, and a Python-based modeling framework (AnalogSim) for benchmarking analog in-memory computing architectures targeting AI/ML acceleration.

Previously, I was a Research Intern at STMicroelectronics (Greater Noida), where I developed behavioral and analytical noise models for SRAM memory subsystems and worked alongside silicon and SoC design teams on memory subsystem characterization. I hold an M.Tech in Electronics Circuit & System Design from Aligarh Muslim University, where I was a UGC-GATE Scholarship recipient.
